Our Partners

OXMK delivers an array of fresh, seasonal fruits grown under ideal conditions to ensure the highest quality. From juicy apples to vibrant berries, they provide a sweet and healthy addition to your meals.

MetaFarm offers a wide range of high-quality meat, including beef, lamb, poultry, and pork. Committed to ethical farming and eco-friendly practices, MetaFarm ensures fresh and flavorful products directly from the farm to your table.

Sun-Bee Altai produces premium organic honey, collected from the pristine forests and meadows of the Altai Mountains. Known for its unique taste and health benefits, their honey is a favorite among locals and a symbol of natural purity.

Rodina Agrofirm is a trusted supplier of high-quality milk, cheese, yogurt, and other dairy products. Located in the heart of Kazakhstan, they take pride in their sustainable farming practices and fresh, nutrient-rich offerings for families across the country.
